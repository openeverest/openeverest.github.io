## Multi-Cloud Freedom

Percona Everest enables you to transcend the limitations of a single cloud provider. Deploy your databases in any Kubernetes cluster, ensuring redundancy, high availability, and data sovereignty.

### Key Benefits

- **Cloud Provider Independence**: Deploy on AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, or any Kubernetes infrastructure
- **High Availability**: Ensure your databases are always accessible with multi-region deployments
- **Data Sovereignty**: Keep your data where you need it to comply with regulations

## Multiple Database Support

Percona Everest offers managed solutions for Percona's open source databases:

- **PostgreSQL**: The world's most advanced open source relational database
- **MySQL**: The most popular open source database
- **MongoDB**: Leading NoSQL database for modern applications
- **More Coming Soon**: Support for additional database engines is in development

## Private Deployments

Take complete control over your database environment:

- **DIY Approach**: Customize every aspect of your database setup
- **Data Privacy**: Your data stays in your infrastructure
- **Security**: Maintain full control over access and security policies
