## Resource Allocation

Handle changing workloads with dynamic resource management.

### On-the-Fly Adjustments

Scale your resources without downtime:

- **CPU Scaling**: Add or reduce processing power as needed
- **Memory Management**: Adjust memory allocation for optimal performance
- **Storage Expansion**: Increase storage capacity when required

### Vertical Scaling

Percona Everest's vertical scaling capabilities ensure you can adapt to changing demands quickly and efficiently.

## Monitoring with PMM

### Percona Monitoring and Management Integration

Gain deep insights into your database performance:

- **Performance Metrics**: Track key performance indicators in real-time
- **Query Analysis**: Identify and optimize slow queries
- **Cluster Health**: Monitor Kubernetes cluster resources
- **Historical Data**: Analyze trends over time
- **Custom Dashboards**: Create views tailored to your needs

### Proactive Management

Stay ahead of issues with:

- **Alerting**: Set up notifications for critical events
- **Trend Analysis**: Identify potential problems before they occur
- **Capacity Planning**: Make informed decisions about resource allocation
