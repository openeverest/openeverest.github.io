## Why stop by?

OpenEverest is a CNCF Sandbox project for unified, vendor-neutral database
provisioning and management on Kubernetes. We'll be at the Project Pavilion
all Thursday afternoon — here's what we'll be talking about:

- **OpenEverest v2 and new providers & plugins** — a deep dive into the v2 release,
  what changed under the hood, and the new database providers and plugin
  architecture that shipped with it.
  → [Read the v2 dev preview announcement](/blog/v2-developer-preview-release/)
- **LFX Mentorship** — the next term is opening up. If you're interested in
  contributing to an open-source CNCF project with real mentorship, come find out
  how to apply.
- **Data on Kubernetes community** — we'll share stats and insights on where the
  community is heading and what patterns we're seeing across production deployments.

We'll have stickers. Bring your questions — and your production war stories.
