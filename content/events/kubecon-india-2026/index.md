## Why stop by?

OpenEverest is a CNCF Sandbox project that brings unified, vendor-neutral
database provisioning and management to any Kubernetes cluster — cloud or
on-prem. If you run stateful workloads (or want to), come talk to us about:

- **v2 Developer Preview** — we recently released the first dev preview of
  OpenEverest v2, a major rework of the platform. Come hear what changed,
  what's coming next, and share your feedback.
  → [Read the v2 dev preview announcement](/blog/v2-developer-preview-release/)
- Running PostgreSQL, MySQL, and MongoDB on Kubernetes with a single control plane
- Backups, restores, and point-in-time recovery across database engines
- Contributing to OpenEverest — including the LFX Mentorship program
- What's on the roadmap, and what you'd like to see

We'll have stickers. Bring your questions — and your war stories.
